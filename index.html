HISTORY

Because of the unique aroma and taste that tobacco gets because from its geographical location, Cuban tobacco is considered to be the best in the world.

Indigenous people in America had been using tobacco before the Spaniard’s arrival. Actually, the word cigar originally derives from the Mayan sikar ("to smoke rolled tobacco leaves" – from si'c, "tobacco"). The Spanish word, "puro" spans the gap between the Mayan and modern use.
